Privacy Policy

Effective May 21, 2026

Dropzona ("we", "us", "our") is an independent product that lets Twitch streamers run automated CS2 skin giveaways and lets viewers receive Steam trade offers when they win. This page explains what data we handle and why.

This policy reflects Dropzona's BETA launch. If we materially change how we handle data, we'll update this page and post a note in our Discord and on X.

What we collect

To run a drop, we collect only what the pipeline needs:

  • For streamers: Twitch account identity via OAuth (username, user ID, channel ID), the Steam account and inventory you connect, the CS2 GSI events your client sends us, and the drop configuration you set up.
  • For viewers: The Twitch username you chat with, the Steam Trade URL you give us, and a record of drops you've been entered into or won.
  • Operational data: IP address, browser, and basic request logs needed to operate the site, keep accounts secure, and prevent abuse.

We do not collect Steam passwords. Steam Mobile Authenticator codes and 2FA are handled by Steam directly — we never see them.

How we use it

  • To route GSI events from CS2 to your dashboard, pick a winner from eligible chat, and build the Steam trade offer.
  • To show streamers their own drop history and account state.
  • To respond to support requests you send us via Discord, X, or Instagram.
  • To detect and prevent abuse of the platform.

We don't sell your personal data. We don't run third-party advertising on Dropzona.

Third parties we rely on

Dropzona is built on top of services provided by:

  • Twitch — OAuth sign-in and chat integration. Their privacy policy applies to data they hold.
  • Steam (Valve) — Steam OpenID, inventory lookup, and Steam trade offers. Their privacy policy applies to data they hold.
  • Vercel — hosting and CDN for this site.

Data retention

We keep account and drop-history data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we delete personally identifying data within 30 days, except where retention is required for fraud prevention or by law.

Your choices

  • You can disconnect Twitch or Steam from your Dropzona account at any time in settings.
  • You can request a copy of your data, or deletion, by contacting us on Discord.
  • You can revoke the Twitch and Steam connections directly with those providers.

Children

Dropzona is not intended for users under the age required by Twitch's and Steam's own terms. Don't use Dropzona if you don't meet those thresholds.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us in Discord or on X.
