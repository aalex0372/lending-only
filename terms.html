What Dropzona is

Dropzona is a tool that lets Twitch streamers automate skin giveaways using CS2 Game State Integration. When a configured in-game event fires, Dropzona selects an eligible viewer from Twitch chat and builds a Steam trade offer to deliver a prize from the streamer's own connected Steam inventory.

Dropzona does not host, own, escrow, or sell skins. All prizes come from the streamer's own Steam inventory, and trades are executed through Steam's official trade-offer flow.
