Common questions
Is this gambling?
No. Viewers never pay anything. Skins come from the streamer's own inventory. There are no tokens, credits, or entry fees — winners are picked at random from eligible viewers in chat.
How do I connect my Steam Trade URL?
Go to your Steam profile → Inventory → Trade Offers → "Who can send me Trade Offers?" → copy the URL. Then paste it in your Dropzona profile settings.
Why didn't I receive my skin?
Check your Steam trade offers — the offer may be pending. If it expired, the skin returns to the streamer's pool. Make sure your Trade URL is correct and your inventory is public.
How do I set up the CS2 Game Agent?
Download the GSI config from the Setup Wizard in your streamer dashboard. Place the file in your CS2 cfg folder. Restart CS2 — the agent connects automatically.
Do I need to link my Twitch account?
Yes. Viewers link Twitch so we can match drops to your chat identity. Streamers link Twitch so Dropzona can read chat events on your channel. It's free and takes a few seconds from your profile.
Is Dropzona affiliated with Valve, Steam, Twitch, or CS2?
No. Dropzona is an independent product. We integrate with official, publicly documented Twitch and Steam interfaces. Valve, Steam, Counter-Strike, Twitch, and Amazon are trademarks of their respective owners.
